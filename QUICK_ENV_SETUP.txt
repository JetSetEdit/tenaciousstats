## Steps to Add in Vercel Dashboard:

1. Go to: https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. Select your project (or create new one)
3. Go to: **Settings** → **Environment Variables**
4. Click **Add New**
5. For each variable:
   - **Key**: `PROPERTY_ID`
   - **Value**: `368035934`
   - **Environments**: ✅ Production ✅ Preview ✅ Development
   - Click **Save**
   
   - **Key**: `GOOGLE_APPLICATION_CREDENTIALS_B64`
   - **Value**: [Paste entire content from `credentials_base64.txt` - it's one long line]
   - **Environments**: ✅ Production ✅ Preview ✅ Development
   - Click **Save**

6. **Important**: After adding variables, you need to redeploy for them to take effect!

## Verify Setup:

After deployment, test the health endpoint:
```
https://your-project.vercel.app/api/health
```

Should return:
```json
{"status": "healthy", "property_id": "368035934"}
```
